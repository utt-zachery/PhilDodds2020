Our Values

No Corporate PAC Money
This Campaign is about people not PACs. Phil cares about cleaning up politics and will create and support legislation that is in your interest instead of corporate interests. People powered government begins with campaign finance, and his campaign embodies it.

Centered Around You
Phil's campaign is about creating a government that works for everyone. With a focus on Liberty, Personal Responsibility, Diversity, Rule, of, by, and for the People, and a commitment to the Education and Encouragement of younger generations, Phil is working to represent you.

Fixing Partisan Gridlock
As an Engineer and a former Independent, Phil's campaign is about bringing people together and solving our biggest problems. While we work hard and provide for our families, we must also look out for each other. Phil is campaigning to find solutions instead of fighting politicts.
