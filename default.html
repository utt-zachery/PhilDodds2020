Our Campaign


No Corporate PAC Money
Phil doesn't just talk about campaign finance, his campaign embodies it. Phil believes in cleaning up our politics and will work to root out corruption every cranny where it exists, even when it its not convinent.

Centered Around You
Phil isn't running for congress to represent billionaires or corporations. Built around an unprecedent level of public input, Phil's campaign is about creating a government that works for everyone.

Fixing Partisan Gridlock
As an engineer and former independent, Phil isn't running to promote radical policies meant to divide the country. Phil's campaign is about bringing people together to solve the biggest problems of our generation.
